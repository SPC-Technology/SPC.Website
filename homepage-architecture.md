# Homepage Architecture

## 1. Homepage Strategy

The homepage should act as the main entry point for first-time visitors evaluating SPC Technology and Phoebus ERP. Its job is to explain, in a few scrolls, that Phoebus ERP is the primary product, what business problems it addresses, and why SPC Technology is a credible implementation partner.

The homepage should support lead generation by doing three things well:

- State the product and company relationship clearly: `Phoebus ERP by SPC Technology`.
- Help different stakeholder types understand the offer quickly without requiring deep navigation.
- Move qualified visitors toward a demo request or direct sales contact.

The homepage should not try to contain every detail. It should summarize the product, establish trust, and direct visitors into the most relevant next step.

## 2. Section Structure

The homepage sections should appear in this order:

1. Hero
2. Business Value
3. Phoebus ERP Overview
4. Core Modules
5. Implementation & Services
6. Technology Credibility
7. Call To Action
8. Footer

## 3. Section Details

### Hero

- **Purpose**: Establish immediately that Phoebus ERP is the main product and that SPC Technology is the company behind it.
- **Content outline**: Product-led headline, short supporting explanation, primary CTA, secondary CTA, optional short proof or positioning line.
- **Suggested headline**: `Phoebus ERP by SPC Technology`
- **Suggested subtext**: `Business software for finance, operations, inventory, purchasing, sales, and reporting, delivered with implementation and support by SPC Technology.`
- **Primary action**: `Request Demo`

### Business Value

- **Purpose**: Explain the practical business outcomes before getting into product structure.
- **Content outline**: Short list of operational and management benefits such as better visibility, process control, reduced duplication, and improved reporting.
- **Suggested headline**: `A Practical ERP Platform for Day-to-Day Business Control`
- **Suggested subtext**: `Phoebus ERP helps businesses manage core operations in one system, with clearer workflows, better reporting, and stronger control across departments.`
- **Primary action**: `Request Demo`

### Phoebus ERP Overview

- **Purpose**: Give a concise overview of what the product is, who it is for, and what it covers.
- **Content outline**: Short product summary, intended business audience, broad process coverage, and a link to the dedicated Phoebus ERP page.
- **Suggested headline**: `What Phoebus ERP Covers`
- **Suggested subtext**: `Phoebus ERP is designed to support the core processes that keep a business running, from finance and accounting to inventory, purchasing, sales, and management reporting.`
- **Primary action**: `Learn More About Phoebus ERP`

### Core Modules

- **Purpose**: Show product breadth in a simple and structured way.
- **Content outline**: High-level module groups only, such as finance, accounting, inventory, purchasing, sales, and reporting. Keep descriptions short and operational.
- **Suggested headline**: `Core Modules`
- **Suggested subtext**: `A modular ERP platform covering the main functions needed to run and monitor business operations.`
- **Primary action**: `View Modules`

### Implementation & Services

- **Purpose**: Show that SPC Technology delivers more than software and can support rollout, customization, and adoption.
- **Content outline**: Implementation support, customization, integration, migration, training, and ongoing assistance. Keep this section focused on delivery capability tied to Phoebus ERP.
- **Suggested headline**: `Implementation, Customization, and Ongoing Support`
- **Suggested subtext**: `SPC Technology helps businesses plan, deploy, adapt, and support Phoebus ERP in real operating environments.`
- **Primary action**: `Contact Sales`

### Technology Credibility

- **Purpose**: Build trust with visitors who need confidence in the company and delivery model.
- **Content outline**: Short company credibility block covering software focus, product ownership, implementation experience, and support continuity. This is not a full About section.
- **Suggested headline**: `Built and Delivered by SPC Technology`
- **Suggested subtext**: `SPC Technology combines product knowledge, implementation capability, and ongoing support to help businesses adopt Phoebus ERP with confidence.`
- **Primary action**: `About SPC Technology`

### Call To Action

- **Purpose**: Give visitors a clear next step after they understand the offer.
- **Content outline**: Short conversion prompt, reminder of product scope, one primary CTA and one secondary CTA.
- **Suggested headline**: `See Whether Phoebus ERP Fits Your Business`
- **Suggested subtext**: `If you are reviewing ERP options or planning a new implementation, start with a product demo or a direct sales conversation.`
- **Primary action**: `Request Demo`

### Footer

- **Purpose**: Provide navigation, support routing, and company/contact access without distracting from the main conversion path.
- **Content outline**: Links to Home, Phoebus ERP, Modules, Services, Support, About, Contact, plus support/helpdesk links and basic contact details.
- **Suggested headline**: `Footer Navigation`
- **Suggested subtext**: `Keep navigation simple and make support access easy for existing customers.`
- **Primary action**: `Contact`

## 4. CTA Strategy

`Request Demo` should be the primary CTA across the homepage. It should appear:

- In the hero section
- After the business value or Phoebus ERP overview section
- In the final CTA section

`Contact Sales` should be the secondary CTA. It should appear:

- In the hero section
- In the implementation and services section
- In the final CTA section

This keeps the conversion structure simple:

- `Request Demo` for product evaluation
- `Contact Sales` for more specific commercial or implementation enquiries

The homepage should avoid too many competing actions. Support-related actions should remain in the footer or support-specific routes rather than sharing prominence with sales CTAs.

## 5. SEO Considerations

### Recommended Keywords

- Phoebus ERP
- Phoebus ERP by SPC Technology
- ERP software
- business software
- ERP implementation
- ERP customization
- accounting and inventory software
- finance and operations software

### Where Keywords Should Appear

- Page title: include `Phoebus ERP` and `SPC Technology`
- Main headline: include `Phoebus ERP by SPC Technology`
- Introductory paragraph: include `ERP software` or `business software`
- Product overview section: include core process terms such as finance, accounting, inventory, purchasing, sales, and reporting
- Services section: include `ERP implementation` and `ERP customization`
- Internal links: use clear anchor text such as `Phoebus ERP`, `Modules`, and `Services`

Keyword use should remain natural. The homepage should prioritize clarity and product understanding over search-volume-driven wording.

## 6. Simplicity Rule

The homepage should remain short, clear, and easy to maintain by a small founder-led team.

To keep it simple:

- Use a limited number of sections with one clear purpose each.
- Keep copy focused on product explanation and buyer understanding.
- Avoid large claim-heavy marketing blocks, complex animations, or dense comparison content.
- Do not overload the homepage with every module, every service, or deep technical detail.
- Use the homepage to guide visitors into the main product and contact paths, not to replace the rest of the site.

For Version 1, the homepage should be treated as a structured overview page with strong conversion intent, not as a full product brochure.
